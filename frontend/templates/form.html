What is this?

This is a simple service that allows you to sign-up and receive an Email of the most recent Tweets from any Twitter user at your desired Frequency.

This service is not affiliated with the company Twitter and simply uses their Public APIs.

This service is provided free of charge with absolutely no guarantees and could disappear at any time for any reason.
